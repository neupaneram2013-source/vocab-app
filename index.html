Ready to find your level?
You'll see 20 words spanning Levels A through G. For each, pick the correct definition from 4 choices. There's no time limit — take your time.
Why take this?
  • Skip what you already know. No point drilling Level A if you're ready for D.
  • Aim at the right challenge. Learning happens fastest at your "edge" — one level up from comfort.
  • Track growth. Retake monthly to see how far you've come.
